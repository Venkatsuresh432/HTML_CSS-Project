Scorpio-N

The Big Daddy of All SUVs

Commanding outside. Intelligent inside.

The All-New Scorpio-N makes every drive an experience with its unmissable design, thrilling performance, advanced technology, intuitive features, superior comfort & safety. It truly is the Big Daddy of SUVs .

Features

1 Take Control With Adrenox

Take Control With Adrenox

Command it all with Adrenox, the intelligent cockpit technology. Immersive experience taken to next level with over 70+ connected car features.

2 Intuitive Infotainment Screen

Intuitive Infotainment Screen

With a 20.3 cm infotainment screen, Daddy’s den is equipped with GPS navigation, Bluetooth, USB, Apple CarPlay & Android Auto. With Daddy connectivity and entertainment, are at your fingertips.

3 Just Ask Alexa

Just Ask Alexa

Use your voice to control features in Daddy’s Den, even from home. Play music, tune-in to the news, check weather, or even order food. Just say, “Alexa, what can you do in the Scorpio-N?”

4 Feel Every Beat With Sony

Feel Every Beat With Sony

Experience 3D sound from 12 Sony speakers placed strategically, for unmatched background score for every drive with Daddy.

5 Reign Every Terrain with 4XPLOR

Reign Every Terrain with 4XPLOR

4XPLOR terrain mode (Normal, Snow, Mud, or Sand) intelligently tailors traction for different terrains along with the most advanced Shift On Fly 4 Wheel Drive.

6 Stay Alert With Driver Drowsiness Detection

Stay Alert With Driver Drowsiness Detection

Keeps you awake for every adventure, the AdrenoX powered Scorpio-N detects your drowsiness and sends you a vibration alert on your steering.

7 Drive Modes For Your Moods - Zip Zap Zoom

Drive Modes For Your Moods - Zip Zap Zoom

Zip for smooth riding especially through traffic. Zap for true Mahindra SUV performance. Zoom to amp it up for a more engaging & exhilarating ride-quality.

8 Navigate With Alexa Enabled

Navigate With Alexa Enabled -what3words

World’s 1st SUV with Alexa enabled ‘what3words’ helps navigate to a precise location using a unique 3-word combination. Daddy takes you places.

Specifications


Engine

Type 1

  • Type : Turbo Petrol Direct Injection (TGDi), mStallion
  • Capacity : 2 L
  • Max. Power : 149.14 kW @ 5000 r/min
  • Max. Torque : 370Nm @ 1750-3000 r/min (MT), 380Nm @ 1750-3000 r/min (AT)
  • Transmission Type : 6 MT / 6 AT

Type 2

  • Type : Diesel Gen II mHawk - Common Rail Direct Injection Technology
  • Capacity : 2.2 L
  • Max. Power : 128.6 kW @ 3500 r/min, 97 kW @ 3750 r/min
  • Max. Torque : 370 Nm @ 1500-3000 r/min (MT), 400 Nm @ 1750-2750 r/min (AT), 300 Nm @1500-3000 r/min (MT)
  • Transmission Type : 6 MT / 6 AT

Dimensions

  • L x W x H : 4662 mm x 1917 mm x 1857 mm
  • Wheelbase : 2750 mm

Suspensions

  • Front Suspension: Double Wishbone Suspension with Coil over Shocks with FDD & MTV-CL
  • Rear Suspension: Pentalink Suspension with WATT's Linkage with FDD & MTV-CL

Brake

  • Ventilated Disc Front & Rear / ESC (Option)

Wheels

  • Alloy Wheels : 255/60 R18 Alloy with Diamond Cut (Z8L)245/65 R17 Alloy with Diamond Cut (Z8)
  • Steel Wheel : 2245/65 R17 Steel (Z2, Z4 & Z6)
  • Spare Wheel : 255/60 R18 Steel (Z8L 4WD), 245/65 R17 Steel (All variants except Z8 L 4WD)

Fuel Tank Capacity

  • 57 Litre
