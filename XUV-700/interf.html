Specifications


Engine

Type 1

  • Type : Turbo Petrol Direct Injection (TGDi), mStallion
  • Capacity : 2 L
  • Max. Power : 147kW @5000 r/min
  • Max. Torque : 380 Nm @ 1750-3000 r/min
  • Transmission Type : 6 MT / 6 AT

Type 2

  • Type : Turbo Diesel with CRDi
  • Capacity : 2.2 L
  • Max. Power : 114 kW @3750 r/min, 136 kW @3500 r/min
  • Max. Torque : 360 Nm @ 1500-2800r/min, 420 Nm @ 1600-2800r/min (MT), 450 Nm @ 1750-2800r/min (AT)
  • Transmission Type : 6 MT / 6 AT

Dimensions

  • L x W x H : 4695 mm x 1890 mm x 1755 mm (with Roof Rail)
  • Wheelbase : 2750 mm

Suspensions

  • Front Suspension: McPherson Strut Independent Suspension with FSD and Stabilizer bar
  • Rear Suspension: Multi-link Independent Suspension with FSD and Stabilizer bar

Brake

  • Front Ventilated Disc / Rear Solid Disc / Electronic Parking Brakes (Option) / ESP (Option)

Wheels

  • Alloy Wheels : 235/60 R18 Alloy with Diamond Cut 235/65 R17 Alloy with Diamond Cut
  • Steel Wheel : 235/65 R17 Steel
  • Spare Wheel : 155/90 R18 Temporary Spare wheel

Fuel Tank Capacity

  • 60 Litre
